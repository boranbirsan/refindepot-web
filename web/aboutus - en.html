ABOUT US

RefinDepot, a Tübitak Martek organization, was established to provide digital services to high school and higher education institutions on global education and career development. RefinDepot; In addition to the online reference letter creation and storage service that gives the company its name, it offers rich information to support the capture of a global perspective in education, through its own portal, to the use of students, teachers and parents. The first project of the company, which was established in June 2019, is for high school students. In this context, the company's objectives are:

  • Administering various tests to clarify the career goals of the students, starting from the first years of high school and renewing each year, and drawing road maps that will facilitate them to reach the determined goals.
  • Providing detailed information aimed at equipping students with educational and non-educational (cultural, artistic, social services, etc.)
  • To help students gain practical experience (with internship) and to document and store these experiences with the help of a digital reference module.

MISSION

To introduce the resources that will provide the equipment required by modern sciences and the elements that have value in the global context to students and parents, to support students in obtaining these equipment and elements, to be open to communication and sharing, with developed artistic sensitivity and skills, diligent, entrepreneurial, creative, innovative, healthy and healthy. To support efforts aimed at providing an environment and opportunity for the growth of happy individuals.

VISION

To serve education systems that train individuals who are ready for life and who strive to add value to themselves and the society they live in.
